What is Bisq Connect?

Bisq Connect is a companion app that lets you monitor and manage your Bisq 2 desktop node from your iPhone. Stay connected to the Bisq peer-to-peer network on the go — check market data, receive push notifications for important events, and communicate with peers through Bisq's private messaging system.

Install via TestFlight

Bisq Connect is distributed through Apple TestFlight, Apple's official platform for beta apps. TestFlight is free, safe, and used by millions of iOS users to access apps before they hit the App Store.

How to install

  1. Get TestFlight - If you don't already have it, download TestFlight from the App Store. It's free.
  2. Tap the install link — Open the link at the top of this page on your iPhone. It will open TestFlight and show Bisq Connect ready to install.
  3. Tap "Install" — Bisq Connect will appear on your home screen just like any other app.
  4. Pair with your node — Open the app and scan the QR code from your Bisq 2 desktop node to connect.
Automatic updates: TestFlight will notify you whenever a new version is available. Updates install with a single tap.

Good to know

  • TestFlight builds expire after 90 days, but we release updates regularly so you'll always have a fresh version.
  • TestFlight supports up to 10,000 testers. For our current community size this is more than enough, but as Bisq grows we plan to expand to additional distribution channels.
  • Your data and settings are preserved across updates.

Requirements

  • iPhone: iOS 16.0 or later
  • Bisq Node: A running Bisq 2 node (desktop or headless) to pair with
  • Network: Local network or Tor access to your node

For a step-by-step setup guide, visit the Bisq Connect Wiki Tutorial.
